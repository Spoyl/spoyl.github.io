Oli Stocks

Founder, Berkana Drums

Summary

Oli is a drummer and mechanical engineer with a background in surgical robotics and consumer products development. He spent three years travelling the world, ultimately becoming a professional drummer in Vietnam. Berkana Drums is the combination of his two biggest passions: music and engineering.

Engineering background

After getting his masters in Mechanical Engineering, Oli spent four years at CMR Surgical, a surgical robotics scale-up based in Cambridge, England. His work ranged from redesigning surgical instruments, to providing technical support in live surgeries. Oli later began freelance work to free up his time for travel. As a contractor, he's done R&D for consumer products and designed manufacturing tools for medical device production lines in Asia. His choice of projects has always been guided by a love for solving novel problems.

Life outside engineering

Before Berkana, Oli travelled across Europe, Asia, and the US doing odd-jobs to support himself; carpentry, farm work, installing solar panels, cooking and housekeeping to name a few. He ended up living in Vietnam working as a drummer with various bands in the Da-Nang area. Whilst there he started several business ventures, including an English language teaching service specifically for engineers (MechSpeak) and a custom guitar accessories company (Twystlabs). He learned a lot about business, marketing, and the realities of running a small company.

The idea for Berkana Drums came from Oli's lived-experience of being a drummer hauling equipment to venues. He set out to design a drumkit that's easy to transport, and sounds great - there was nothing on the market that did both. Now, there is.
