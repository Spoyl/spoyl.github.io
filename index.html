What it is

A 3-piece birch shell pack — 16" kick, 13" floor tom, 10" high tom — with our patent pending stacking system. Beautiful sound, and a better way to get around.

ᚱ

How it works

We designed a stable, quick-release mechanism on the resonant head of the kick. The head comes off in seconds. Toms drop inside the kick. Everything fits in a single case. Tune as normal with a standard drum key.

ᚦ

Why it exists

Portable kits sound off. Pro kits don't move. There isn't a third option — so we built Berkana. The market has great compact hardware sets, but no great compact shell sets. With Berkana you have a complete professional rig in a single case.
