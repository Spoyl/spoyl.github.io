About

I'm Oli; an ex surgical robotics engineer turned travelling drummer. After gigging in Vietnam for over a year, I realised that drumkits fall into two categories: sound great but aren't portable; or sound terrible but are portable. I'm setting out to design a drumkit that's easy to transport, without compromising on quality.

This is a big project, and something I'm very excited about. I've been told many times to share the journey, so that's what I'm doing; on YouTube and in this newsletter.

If you want to follow the build, sign up to the newsletter. I'll be sharing updates on the design process, prototype testing and all the ups and downs of building a product from scratch.
