The problem we solved
01

Portable kits suck

Every portable drumkit on the market makes trade-offs: flimsy hardware, split shells, single heads... You get something that travels — but it doesn't sound like a real drumkit.

02

Professional kits don't move

Professional-grade kits — the kind that actually sound right in a venue — aren't portable. Even "compact" kits take up to 4 trips to transport. Hardware alone fills a separate bag.

03

We built a third option

Quality hardware that folds to 2 inches in diameter. Quick-release lugs that let toms stack inside the kick. Birch shells that sound beautiful. All in a single flight case.
