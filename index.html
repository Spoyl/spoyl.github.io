What it is

A 4-piece birch shell pack with a patent pending stacking system — 16" kick, 14"snare, 13" floor tom, 10" high tom. Beautiful sound, and easy to get around! If you want to build your own, we can send you the 3D print files.

ᚱ

How it works

A patent pending quick-release mechanism on the resonant head of the kick allows the head to come off in seconds. Toms stack inside the kick, and everything fits in a single case (tune as normal with a standard drum key).

ᚦ

Why it exists

Portable kits sound bad and standard kits don't move. There hasn't been a viable third option until now. With Berkana you have a great-sounding, professional shell-set in a single case.
