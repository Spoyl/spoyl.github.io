About

Hi, I'm Oli - an ex surgical robotics engineer from the UK turned travelling drummer. After playing music professionally in Vietnam for over a year, I realised that drumkits fall into two categories: sound great but aren't portable; or portable but don't sound great. I'm setting out to design a drumkit that's easy to transport, without compromising on quality.

This is an ambitious project, and I have no idea how it'll turn out.

I'll be sharing updates on the design, and all the ups and downs of building Berkana from scratch in this newsletter and on my YouTube channel. There should be something for drummers and makers alike. Sign up to follow along.
