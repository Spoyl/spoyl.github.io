How it works

It's a simple over-centre latch, with an additional joint to make it work smoothly. Tension from the head holds it shut. Lift the handle to open. Tune as normal.

ᚱ

Why I'm sharing it

Manufacturing costs for full drum kits are high, but I like this design so I'm putting it out there to see what people do with it.

ᚦ

The bigger project

Regular drums aren't portable portable enough, so I designed this latch as a way to stack my toms into my kick. If there's loads of interest, I can justify making and selling a full kit based on it.
